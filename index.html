About Me

My name is Isaac CINZA, from the Democratic Republic of Congo. I am passionate about education, technology, and sharing knowledge. My journey began with medicine, where I learned discipline and empathy. Medicine taught me the value of rigorous study and responsibility. I also pursued journalism, discovering the power of storytelling. Journalism sharpened my voice and ability to inform communities. It gave me tools to connect people through ideas and narratives. Later, I joined BYU‑Pathway to study software development. Web development opened doors to creativity and innovation. I enjoy building webpages that make information accessible. Technology, for me, bridges health, education, and communication. My hybrid path combines science, media, and digital skills. I aspire to lead in digital health and science communication. My goal is to empower communities through knowledge and innovation. This journey reflects my belief that learning transforms lives.
